# Creating an Account

To begin using the Agent Tool Protocol (ATP), you'll need to create an account on our platform.

## Sign Up Process

1. Navigate to the [ChatATP](https://chat-atp.com)
2. Click the "Chat with Agent" or "Sign Up" button
3. Enter your email address and choose a secure password
4. Verify your email address through the confirmation link
5. Complete the onboarding process to set up your account

## Account Verification

After signing up, you'll receive a verification email. Click the verification link to activate your account. This step is required before you can access the platform features.

## First Login

Once your account is verified:

1. Return to the platform login page
2. Enter your email and password
3. Complete the initial setup wizard
4. You're ready to start configuring your agents!

## Next Steps

Now that you have an account, you can proceed to [connect your LLM provider](/account-setup/connecting-llm-provider) to start building intelligent agents.
